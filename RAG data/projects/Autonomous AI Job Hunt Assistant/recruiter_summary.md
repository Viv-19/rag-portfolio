# Recruiter Summary — Autonomous AI Job Hunt Assistant

## Project Overview

The Autonomous AI Job Hunt Assistant is a locally hosted AI-powered workflow automation platform designed to automate and manage job hunting activities using autonomous agents, workflow orchestration, and Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) approval systems.

The platform combines:
- AI reasoning
- workflow automation
- API orchestration
- email drafting
- interview scheduling
- audit logging

into a single intelligent productivity system.

The project was designed with a strong focus on:
- safe AI automation
- human oversight
- resilient workflow execution
- modular orchestration
- secure integrations

rather than fully uncontrolled AI execution.

---

## Human-in-the-Loop (HITL) Safety Design

One of the most important engineering decisions was implementing a:
```text
Draft First, Send Second
```

approval workflow.

The AI assistant can:
- generate drafts
- recommend actions
- automate workflows

but cannot execute sensitive operations like sending emails without explicit user approval.

This significantly improves:
- safety
- trust
- controllability
- operational reliability

---
