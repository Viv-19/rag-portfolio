# Career Goals

## Short-Term Goals

My short-term goal is to join a strong engineering-focused company where I can learn how large-scale systems are designed, built, deployed, and maintained in production environments. I want to work alongside experienced engineers and learn from senior developers who have deep practical knowledge of software engineering and AI systems.

I want to strengthen my understanding of:
- scalable backend systems
- production AI architecture
- distributed systems
- cloud infrastructure
- engineering workflows
- team collaboration
- system design at scale

At this stage of my career, learning and gaining real-world engineering experience is my highest priority.

## Long-Term Vision

Once I gain strong experience and a deep understanding of systems, products, and engineering leadership, I want to build something of my own.

My long-term goal is to start a technology company focused on building impactful AI-driven products that solve meaningful real-world problems and create positive impact for society.

I do not just want to build technology for trends — I want to create systems and products that are genuinely useful, scalable, and capable of helping people at a larger level.

## Dream Role

My dream role is to become:
- a strong AI engineer
- a high-level system builder
- a developer who understands products deeply
- a technical leader who guides teams
- someone who creates meaningful impact through technology

I want to combine:
- deep technical skills
- product thinking
- leadership
- innovation
- execution

into one career path.

## Professional Direction

I see myself growing in the direction of:
- AI Engineering
- Generative AI
- Autonomous AI Systems
- Backend and Infrastructure Engineering
- Product Engineering
- Technical Leadership

I want to continuously evolve from an individual contributor into someone who can design systems, lead teams, and build products that matter.
